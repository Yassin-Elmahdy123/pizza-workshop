How to make mashed potatoes

Mashed Potatoes is a very tasty and creamy meal for breakfast, lunch, or dinner.

You can make mashed potatoes through different ways.

But I will talk about my favourite way to make it.

First you need to gather these ingredients:

  • 2 big-sized potatoes or 3 medium-sized potatoes.
  • 1 big spoon full of butter.
  • 3 tbsp of salt, black pepper, cumin, and hot pepper

Second you need to do these steps in order:

  1. Put your potatoes in a pot containing boiling water.
  2. while waiting for the potatoes to be cooked, put your spices and butter in the serving plate.
  3. You will know that the potatoes are cooked when you are able to enter a fork in the potatoes easily.
  4. drain the potatoes from the boiling water.
  5. Put the potatoes in the serving plate.
  6. Mash the potatoes while mixing them to spread all the spices and butter evenly.
  7. Eat with bread and have fun.
